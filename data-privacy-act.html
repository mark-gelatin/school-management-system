Data Privacy Notice and Consent Form for Students

Data Privacy Act (R.A. 10173)

Colegio de Amore is required by law to process your personal information in accordance with Republic Act No. 10173, otherwise known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012. This notice explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data for admission and enrollment purposes.

Personal Information Collected and Stored

The Colegio de Amore collects, uses and keeps your personal information, which include but are not limited, to the following:

  • Name, age, date of birth
  • Address and contact details
  • Civil Status, Nationality, Religion
  • Family Profile (Names of parents/guardians, their occupation and contact details, etc.)
  • Choice of degree program
  • Educational records
  • Learner reference number (if any)
  • Geographic information about your household

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Processing your admission application
  • Creating and maintaining your student record
  • Communicating with you about your application and enrollment
  • Providing academic services and support
  • Maintaining academic records and transcripts
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure database systems
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates

Your Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have the following rights:

  • Right to be informed: You have the right to know how your data is being processed
  • Right to access: You can request access to your personal data
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your data
  • Right to erasure: You can request the deletion of your data
  • Right to data portability: You can request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data

Important: By proceeding with your application, you consent to the collection, processing, and storage of your personal information as described in this privacy notice. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting our Data Protection Officer.
