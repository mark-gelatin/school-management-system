President's Message

A message from the President of Colegio De Amore, sharing our vision and commitment to excellence in education.

Mrs. Leticia F. Villanueva

President, Colegio De Amore

Welcome to Colegio De Amore, where excellence, love, and virtue form the foundation of our educational mission.

As we continue to navigate the ever-evolving landscape of education, I am proud to lead an institution that remains steadfast in its commitment to providing quality education that nurtures not only the mind but also the heart and spirit of every student.

At Colegio De Amore, we believe that education is more than just the transmission of knowledge. It is about shaping character, fostering critical thinking, and preparing our students to become responsible citizens who will contribute meaningfully to society.

Our dedicated faculty and staff work tirelessly to create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and personally. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of education while adapting to the changing needs of our students and the world around us.

I invite you to explore our programs and discover how Colegio De Amore can help you or your loved ones achieve their educational goals. Together, let us continue to build a brighter future through education.
