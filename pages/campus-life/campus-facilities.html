Campus Facilities

Discover our modern and well-equipped facilities designed to support your academic journey and enhance your learning experience.

Academic Facilities

Computer Laboratories

State-of-the-art computer labs equipped with the latest hardware and software for programming, research, and digital learning.

Features:

  • High-speed internet connection
  • Latest software applications
  • Air-conditioned environment
  • Projector and audio systems
Operating Hours: Monday - Friday, 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Library and Learning Center

Comprehensive library with extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support academic research.

Features:

  • Extensive book collection
  • Digital resources and databases
  • Quiet study areas
  • Computer workstations
Operating Hours: Monday - Saturday, 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Science Laboratories

Well-equipped science labs for chemistry, biology, and physics experiments with modern equipment and safety features.

Features:

  • Modern laboratory equipment
  • Safety protocols and equipment
  • Ventilation systems
  • Storage for chemicals and specimens
Operating Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Smart Classrooms

Modern classrooms equipped with smart boards, projectors, and audio-visual systems for interactive learning.

Features:

  • Interactive smart boards
  • HD projectors
  • Audio-visual systems
  • Comfortable seating
Operating Hours: Monday - Friday, 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Sports and Recreation

Gymnasium

Multi-purpose gymnasium for basketball, volleyball, and other indoor sports activities.

Features:

  • Basketball court
  • Volleyball court
  • Sound system
  • Seating for spectators
Operating Hours: Monday - Friday, 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Outdoor Sports Area

Open-air sports facilities including football field, running track, and outdoor courts for various sports.

Features:

  • Football field
  • Running track
  • Outdoor basketball court
  • Table tennis area
Operating Hours: Daily, 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Student Services

Cafeteria

Clean and spacious cafeteria offering a variety of meals, snacks, and beverages at affordable prices.

Features:

  • Variety of food options
  • Clean dining area
  • Affordable prices
  • Student-friendly menu
Operating Hours: Monday - Friday, 7:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Student Center

Central hub for student activities, meetings, and social gatherings with comfortable seating and meeting rooms.

Features:

  • Meeting rooms
  • Lounge areas
  • Student organization offices
  • Activity space
Operating Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Health Center

On-campus health center providing basic medical services, first aid, and health consultations for students.

Features:

  • Basic medical services
  • First aid supplies
  • Health consultations
  • Emergency response
Operating Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Parking Area

Secure parking facilities for students, faculty, and visitors with designated areas for motorcycles and cars.

Features:

  • Secure parking spaces
  • Motorcycle parking
  • Visitor parking
  • Security monitoring
Operating Hours: Daily, 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Specialized Facilities

Criminology Laboratory

Specialized laboratory for criminology students with forensic equipment and crime scene investigation tools.

Features:

  • Forensic equipment
  • Crime scene simulation
  • Evidence collection tools
  • Analysis workstations
Operating Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Hospitality Training Center

Professional training facility for hospitality management students with commercial-grade kitchen and service areas.

Features:

  • Commercial kitchen
  • Service training area
  • Restaurant simulation
  • Professional equipment
Operating Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Facility Usage Guidelines

General Rules

  • Present valid student ID when using facilities
  • Follow posted operating hours
  • Maintain cleanliness and order
  • Report any damages or issues immediately
  • Respect other users and staff

Reservation Policy

  • Advance booking required for special activities
  • Submit reservation requests at least 48 hours in advance
  • Provide detailed activity information
  • Follow facility-specific guidelines
  • Clean up after use

Safety Requirements

  • Follow all safety protocols
  • Use protective equipment when required
  • Report safety hazards immediately
  • Participate in safety orientations
  • Follow emergency procedures
