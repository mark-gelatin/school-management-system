Starting a New Club

Don't see a club that matches your interests? You can start your own! Here's what you need to do:

Minimum Requirements

  • At least 10 interested members
  • Faculty advisor
  • Club constitution and bylaws
  • Proposed activities and goals

Application Process

  • Submit club proposal
  • Present to Student Affairs Office
  • Get approval from administration
  • Complete registration process
