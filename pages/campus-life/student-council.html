Election Process

1

Nomination Period

Students can nominate themselves or be nominated by peers for council positions.

February 1-15, 2025
2

Campaign Period

Candidates campaign and present their platforms to the student body.

February 16-28, 2025
3

Election Day

March 1, 2025

Results & Inauguration

March 5, 2025

How to Get Involved

Run for Office

Consider running for a student council position during the next election.

  • Meet eligibility requirements
  • Submit nomination form
  • Prepare campaign materials
  • Present your platform

Join Committees

Participate in various council committees and working groups.

  • Academic Affairs Committee
  • Student Activities Committee
  • Community Service Committee
  • Communications Committee

Attend Meetings

Participate in open council meetings and voice your opinions.

  • Monthly general assemblies
  • Committee meetings
  • Special sessions
  • Public forums
