Admission Steps for New Students

Follow these step-by-step instructions to complete your admission process and begin your educational journey at Colegio De Amore.

Admission Process

1

Submit Application Form

Complete the online application form or obtain a physical copy from the admissions office. Ensure all information is accurate and complete.

Required Information:

  • Personal Information
  • Educational Background
  • Program of Choice
  • Contact Details
2

Submit Required Documents

Gather and submit all required documents to the admissions office. Documents must be original or certified true copies.

Required Documents:

  • High School Diploma or Equivalent
  • Official Transcript of Records
  • Birth Certificate (PSA)
  • 2x2 ID Photos (4 copies)
  • Medical Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
3

Pay Application Fee

Pay the non-refundable application fee at the cashier's office or through designated payment channels.

Payment Options:

  • Cash payment at cashier's office
  • Bank deposit/transfer
  • Online payment (if available)
4

Take Entrance Examination

Schedule and take the entrance examination. The exam covers general knowledge, English, Mathematics, and logical reasoning.

Exam Coverage:

  • General Knowledge
  • English Proficiency
  • Mathematics
  • Logical Reasoning
5

Interview

Participate in an interview with the admissions committee. This is an opportunity to discuss your goals and demonstrate your commitment to education.

Interview Topics:

  • Educational Goals
  • Career Aspirations
  • Why Choose Colegio De Amore
  • Personal Motivation
6

Receive Admission Decision

Wait for the admission decision. Results will be communicated through your preferred contact method within 5-7 business days.

Decision Notification:

  • Email notification
  • Phone call
  • Official letter
  • Online portal update
7

Complete Enrollment

If accepted, complete the enrollment process by submitting additional requirements and paying the necessary fees.

Enrollment Requirements:

  • Acceptance letter
  • Enrollment form
  • Tuition fee payment
  • Student ID application

Important Dates

Application Period

January 1 - March 31, 2025

Submit your application and required documents during this period.

Entrance Examination

February 1 - April 15, 2025

Schedule and take your entrance examination.

Interview Period

March 1 - April 30, 2025

Complete your interview with the admissions committee.

Enrollment Period

May 1 - June 30, 2025

Complete enrollment for accepted students.
